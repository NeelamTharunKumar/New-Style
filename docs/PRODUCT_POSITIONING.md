# Product Positioning

## Working name

**Drape AI**

This is a working product name. It can be changed later, but the repo should avoid the previous `StyleDNA` naming because that overlaps with existing fashion products.

## Category

Privacy-first AI wardrobe assistant for Indian wardrobes.

## Main differentiation

Most global wardrobe apps are Western-fashion-first. Drape AI focuses on Indian realities:

- ethnic + western combinations
- Haldi, Sangeet, Mehendi, pooja, reception and wedding guest outfits
- Indian college and office dressing
- hot/humid/monsoon weather
- saree/blouse/dupatta/kurti styling
- practical men’s styling
- budget-conscious “use what you own” recommendations
- privacy-first structured-data architecture

## Not the positioning

Do not position this as just:

- “AI tells you which color looks good”
- “generic AI fashion chatbot”
- “shopping recommendation app”
- “virtual try-on-first app”

## Strong positioning statement

> Drape AI helps Indian users decide what to wear from their own wardrobe for college, office, dates, weddings and daily life — with privacy-first on-device photo handling.

## MVP wedge

The first wedge should be:

> Upload or manually add wardrobe items. Ask for an occasion. Get exact outfit combinations from your own clothes.

## Future wedge

After the outfit loop works:

- local AI feature extraction
- LLM explanations
- wear history
- capsule wardrobe insights
- wedding function planner
- travel packing lists
