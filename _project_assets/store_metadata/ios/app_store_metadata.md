# App Store Metadata Draft

## App name

Drape AI

## Subtitle

Outfits from your own wardrobe

## Promotional text

India-first AI wardrobe assistant for college, office, dates, weddings and daily dressing.

## Description

Drape AI helps Indian users create outfit combinations from clothes they already own. Add wardrobe items, choose an occasion, and get outfit ideas with explanations for why each combination works.

Designed for ethnic + western wardrobes, men's practical styling, Indian weather and events like Haldi, Sangeet, pooja, festivals and wedding guest looks.

Privacy-first approach: wardrobe photos are designed to stay on-device by default while structured clothing features power recommendations.

## Keywords

wardrobe,outfit,closet,AI stylist,Indian fashion,kurti,saree,menswear,wedding,college

## Privacy nutrition notes

- User ID/authentication data may be collected.
- Structured wardrobe attributes may be processed.
- Raw photos are designed to stay on-device by default.
- Data deletion/export available through backend lifecycle endpoints.
